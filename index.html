NFL RB Fantasy Points by Season

In Fantasy Football, a team in a standard league starts 2 running backs (RB) every week. In a league with 'n' teams (usually 10 or 12) the top 'n' RB's are considered an "RB1" (good enough to be the first starting back on any team) and the next 'n' are "RB2" (the rest of the running backs are considered not good enough to be an ideal starter). Using D3.js and my longest running Fantasy League's scoring system I plotted the final fantasy points of the season for each RB and overlayed the average fantasy points per season of an RB1 or RB2

Every dot represents an individual RB in that season that attempted at least 20 runs. Hover your cursor over the dot to see the name of the player and total Fantasy points
